Hyderabadi Biryani

Description: A regal dish from the royal kitchens of Hyderabad, this biryani is known for its aromatic basmati rice, tender marinated meat (chicken), and a perfect blend of spices. Cooked in the traditional dum style, it offers a rich, flavorful experience.

Ingredients: Basmati rice, meat (chicken), yogurt, fried onions, saffron, ghee, and a special blend of spices.

Kolkata Biryani

Description: A unique biryani with a hint of sweetness, Kolkata biryani is distinguished by the addition of potatoes and boiled eggs. It's a light and aromatic dish that reflects the culinary traditions of Bengal.

Ingredients: Basmati rice, meat (chicken, mutton, or beef), potatoes, boiled eggs, yogurt, ghee, and a delicate mix of spices.

Lucknowi Biryani

Description: Originating from the Awadhi cuisine of Lucknow, this biryani is known for its subtle flavors and rich texture. The meat is cooked separately and then layered with fragrant rice, making it a true delicacy.

Vegetable Biryani

Description: A delightful option for vegetarians, this biryani is packed with fresh vegetables and fragrant spices. It's a wholesome and satisfying meal that doesn't compromise on flavor.

Ingredients: Basmati rice, mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, potatoes, beans), yogurt, fried onions, saffron, and a blend of spices.
